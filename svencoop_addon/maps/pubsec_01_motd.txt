Welcome to Public Security.

On this map, you will have to clear a bar (the yellow brick building) that belongs to an armed gang. 
Your objectives are:
========================================================

- Take down the leader of the gang (the guy with the Uzi)

- Kill all enemies

- Locate the drugs

========================================================

Grab the weapons of your choice in the armory by pressing your USE key on them.

Warning: Reloading will empty your clip.
You can carry only one primary weapon at a time.
The Shotgun has to be pumped manually by pressing the Secondary Fire key.

This map is balanced for 8-10 players.
